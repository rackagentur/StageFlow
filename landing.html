FAQ

Questions DJs ask
before they start.

Is NoxReach actually live and being used?
Yes — NoxReach is a live product used by DJs across Tech-House, Circuit, and Disco scenes. It's actively developed with updates based on real user feedback. You can sign up and start using it today.
How is this different from a spreadsheet or Notion?
Spreadsheets and Notion don't tell you what to do next. NoxReach surfaces due follow-ups automatically, tracks lead stages, and gives you a daily view of who needs contact. It's a system, not just a database.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data stays intact. On the free plan everything remains accessible. On paid plans, premium tools become unavailable after your term ends, but your leads and history are not deleted.
Do I need a credit card to start?
No credit card required for the free plan. If you choose a paid plan, billing starts when you upgrade — not before. Cancel anytime from your account settings.
Is the free plan actually enough to try it?
Yes. The free plan gives you enough leads to run your first 2–3 outreach campaigns and experience exactly how the pipeline and follow-up system work. Most DJs who try it upgrade within their first month.
Which plan is right for me?
Choose Free to get organised and test the workflow. Choose Pro if you're actively booking and need automated follow-ups and unlimited leads. Choose Team if you manage outreach with a booking partner.
